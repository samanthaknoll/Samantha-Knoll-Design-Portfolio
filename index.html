What I Offer

Custom eLearning Solutions
I specialize in creating custom eLearning content that meet action-oriented objectives and audiences needs by blending course authoring tools with custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript web development. Skilled in authoring tools like Lectora and Articulate Storyline, I also use Canva and Camtasia for visual aids, GIFs, and videos, ensuring each solution aligns perfectly with both customer and organizational goals.
Instructional Design Expertise
My instructional design expertise is deeply anchored in learning theories and the practical application of models like ADDIE and SAM. I infuse storytelling into the learning journey, making complex concepts accessible and relatable, and ensuring each course meets clients at their current level of understanding. I focus on meeting clients' needs through reflective and challenging learning journeys to achieve true behavior-change.
Interactive Content Development
My proficiency lies in crafting interactive content that elevates learner engagement and retention, including dynamic quizzes, branching scenarios, interactive video, simulations, and gamified experiences. I specialize in creating responsive, mobile-friendly courses for an exceptional experience on any device. Committed to ADA compliance and accessibility standards, I cater to a diverse range of abilities and needs.

Instructional Design Process

Needs Analysis

In conducting a needs analysis, I first engage with key stakeholders and target audiences to identify and understand their specific challenges and objectives. Utilizing tools like surveys, interviews, and data analysis, I gather and assess information to pinpoint knowledge gaps. This process culminates in a detailed deliverable that outlines the problem areas, learner profiles, and recommended training solutions.

Script and Storyboard

In script writing, I focus on crafting relatable stories and real-world scenarios that resonate with learners. My scripts are designed for scenario-based learning, immersing learners in practical situations. Additionally, when creating storyboards, I prioritize the user-experience, visual design, and mobile responsiveness.

Development

In the development phase, I bring storyboards to life, transforming them into interactive and immersive eLearning experiences. By integrating native LMS elements, course authoring tools, and video development resources, I create multifaceted learning modules. Additionally, I employ HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to enhance interactivity and ensure a seamless and responsive design across platforms.

Evaluation

In analyzing course data, I conduct a comprehensive review that encompasses customer satisfaction, asset engagement, and learner feedback. I focus on key metrics like enrollment trends, completion rates, and seat time accuracy, while also considering learner penetration and the effectiveness of interactive elements. This approach enables me to identify areas for improvement, validate successful strategies, and continuously enhance the overall quality and relevance of eLearning content.
